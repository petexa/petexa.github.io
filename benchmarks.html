Hero WODs

Murph

For Time
For time:
  • 1 Mile Run
  • 100 Pull-ups
  • 200 Push-ups
  • 300 Air Squats
  • 1 Mile Run
Partition the pull-ups, push-ups, and squats as needed. Performed with 9kg/6kg weight vest (Rx).
Elite (Rx): < 35:00
Advanced (Rx): 35:00-45:00
Intermediate: 45:00-60:00

DT

For Time
5 rounds for time:
  • 12 Deadlifts (70kg/50kg)
  • 9 Hang Power Cleans
  • 6 Push Jerks
Use same barbell for all movements. No resting the bar on the ground during a round.
Elite: < 7:00
Advanced: 7:00-10:00
Intermediate: 10:00-15:00

Classic Tests

Fight Gone Bad

3 Rounds
3 rounds, 1 min each station:
  • Wall Balls (9kg/6kg)
  • SDHP (35kg/25kg)
  • Box Jumps (20")
  • Push Press (35kg/25kg)
  • Row (Calories)
  • 1 min Rest
Score is total reps across all rounds.
Elite: 350+ points
Advanced: 300-350 points
Intermediate: 250-300 points

Filthy Fifty

For Time
50 reps each, for time:
  • Box Jumps (24"/20")
  • Jumping Pull-ups
  • KB Swings (16kg)
  • Walking Lunges
  • Knees to Elbows
  • Push Press (20kg)
  • Back Extensions
  • Wall Balls (9kg/6kg)
  • Burpees
  • Double Unders
Elite: < 20:00
Advanced: 20:00-30:00
Intermediate: 30:00-40:00
